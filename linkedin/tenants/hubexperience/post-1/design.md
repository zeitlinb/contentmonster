# Design — The HUB Experience Company (LinkedIn first-post image)

## Palette
| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| sage | `#667263` | Dominant field (selected LinkedIn accent) |
| sage-deep | `#55604F` | Vignette / depth within the sage family |
| cream | `#F1E8D9` | Primary text, emblem, hairlines |
| cream-lift | `#F7F0E3` | Small text needing extra contrast |
| ink | `#1A1208` | Reserved (not on this dark field) |
| muted-cream | `#DCE1D6` | Quiet secondary text |

## Typography
- **Display:** Fraunces (SemiBold ~600) — the hero "We are Memory Makers."
- **Sans:** Geist (Medium/SemiBold) — eyebrow, essence line, footer. All-caps + tracked for labels.
- No mono. No substitutions.

## Register
Editorial, premium, hospitality/experiential (Aman / Soho House restraint × Live Nation weight). Flat and warm — hairlines + generous negative space carry hierarchy.

## What NOT to do
- No AI-blue/purple, no SaaS gradients, no neon, no pastels.
- No pure white (`#FFFFFF`) or pure black (`#000000`).
- No shadows/glassmorphism/elevation cards. No stock-photo cliché.
- No "launching / startup" energy. Operator-grade, not launcher-grade.
