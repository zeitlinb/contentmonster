## Why LinkedIn is different from HEC's IG/FB
LinkedIn is where HEC's actual buyers are (the 10 archetypes), in decision-maker mode. So the register skews **editorial and operator-grade**, the outcomes are **business-case first**, and the CTA stays the locked **"Get in touch."** This is the surface for the **Deeper Layer** (the Memory Channel ROI argument) more than the IG "front door" — long-form thought-leadership belongs here (per `brand-guidelines.md` §4).

## Cadence & register
- **2–3 posts/week** sustained (matches the locked social cadence).
- **Register:** confident, restrained, mature; no hype, no launcher voice, no objection-overcoming. State facts; let the buyer decide.
- **One idea per post.** Single strong visual + tight copy + one CTA ("Get in touch" → hubexperienceco.com).

## Pillars (LinkedIn-tuned)
1. **Memory Makers / methodology** — the manifesto + the infuse/ingredient principle; what "operators, not decorators" means.
2. **Proof points** — stats and case results as they accrue (e.g., comp-set outperformance, PSF, absorption). The corpus IS the credibility.
3. **The Memory Channel (business case)** — long-form, category-defining ROI argument for CMOs/CEOs (revenue, absorption, retention). LinkedIn's highest-leverage lane.
4. **Who we serve** — archetype-specific angles (mixed-use, hotels, cities, multifamily, retail, festivals, brands).
5. **Behind the work** — real HUB event execution / activation in motion (photo-led, blue-hour/golden-hour, real people).

## CTA pattern
One CTA per post: **"Get in touch" → hubexperienceco.com** (UTM-tag if tracking). Never "like & follow" as the ask. No published pricing, no "book a call/sprint" SaaS phrasing.

## Measurement (not vanity)
Judge on **qualified inbound + page/profile visits from target archetypes** (developers, owners, asset managers, cities), and conversations started — not likes/impressions. Leading indicators: follows by decision-makers at target companies; profile-to-site clicks. One inbound from a real developer > thousands of impressions.
