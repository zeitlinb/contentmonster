# Constellation — YouTube content strategy (PROPOSAL v1)

> **Status:** core **LOCKED 2026-07-01** (see *Locked decisions* below); a few production refinements remain (bottom). Built on **weekly** cadence + **hybrid faceless-first** (3:1), pressure-tested against live 2026 research on the AI-visibility/GEO landscape and run through an adversarial editorial pass. Source of truth for values = [`channel-config.md`](channel-config.md).

## Locked decisions (2026-07-01)
- **Cadence:** weekly. **Ratio:** 3:1 faceless:founder.
- **Founder (Henry on-camera):** the **case study (W3)** + **Two Machines (W6)** — the two highest-conversion moments.
- **Lead magnet:** the **"Where are you invisible?" AI-visibility scan** — single low-friction offer; must be built + fast/mobile-clean before W1 ships.
- **Lane:** **high-ticket B2B only** — skip local/"near me" + Amazon/Rufus deep-dives on this channel (reserve those for tenants with a commerce/local surface).

## The channel's promise (positioning hook)
Every video shows a business owner exactly where AI search is quietly sending their next buyer to a competitor — and how to turn that discovery back into revenue. You leave knowing **where you're invisible, what it's costing, and the first move to fix it.**

## Operating rhythm (weekly, hybrid faceless-first)
- **Cadence:** 1 video/week. **Ratio 3:1** faceless:founder — 3 faceless HyperFrames explainers to 1 Henry founder piece per month (founder ≈ every 4th week).
- **Formats:** two **hero/awareness** shorts (60–90s, faceless, legible on mute) seed the top of funnel; the rest are **8–15 min lead-gen deep-dives**. Shorts only ever tease → route to long-form; never the lead engine.
- **Pillar rotation:** no two consecutive weeks share a pillar; every month touches the revenue thesis ≥2×. Founder weeks reserved for the highest-trust, offer-adjacent pieces (case study, Two-Machines).
- **Production:** monthly batches. Brad/Henry approve the month's 4 titles + hooks + CTAs in one sitting → agents draft scripts + build the 3 faceless explainers (ElevenLabs "Mark" clone, kinetic-type/data-viz) → Henry records the single founder piece in one block. Weekly cadence stays sustainable on agent labor + one monthly on-camera session.

## Pillars
1. **Hero / Awareness (search *evolves*)** — 60–90s faceless entry points on the loudest buyer panics (traffic drop, "search moved"); reframes fear as a revenue signal; Google listed first, search evolves (never "Google is dying").
2. **AI-Engine Deep Dives (the 6+ places)** — the evergreen backbone: one 8–15 min faceless deep-dive per engine (Google AI first, then ChatGPT, Perplexity/Claude, Amazon, local). Double-ranks on Google + YouTube; the scalable weekly workhorse + compounding lead library.
3. **Anonymized Case Studies (the citation gap)** — sharpest "start where it hurts": a category, the competitor named in the answer vs the one who isn't, and the quantified lost-revenue math. Highest buyer-intent; strongest qualified-application driver. Never names clients; **often founder-led**.
4. **Founder Thought Leadership (Henry on camera)** — premium-trust, offer-adjacent pieces on the highest-altitude messages; builds the parasocial trust a high-ticket sale needs; ~monthly.
5. **Methodology / Proof (34+ bots, measurement)** — demonstrates the Presence + Revenue Machine as a real system; owns the measurement white-space; justifies premium engagement.

## Title & thumbnail conventions (lock before video #1)
- **Titles:** keyword + concrete **revenue outcome** in the first 3–5 words; <60 chars; **never** "rank better" or "AI SEO explained"; max one emphasized word, no ALL-CAPS/power-word spam; don't date evergreen pieces; Google named first.
- **Thumbnails:** dark premium ground (navy/near-black), **exactly one accent color** (warm/gold or a signal accent — never AI-blue/purple), one 3–4 word revenue promise over heavy negative space, one focal visual. Faceless → motion-graphic key visual (citation node, split search bar, revenue line); founder → one calm Henry portrait + 3–4 words. No shocked faces, no arrows — the restraint *is* the scroll-stop for execs.

## CTA pattern
One primary CTA per video, hard-wired into the **first line of the description** (first ~200 chars = one-sentence summary + single CTA + tracked link), mirrored on end screen, cards, pinned comment. Never "like & subscribe" as the ask. **Two tiers by funnel position:** awareness/deep-dive/methodology → low-friction lead magnet (the AI-visibility "scan"); case-study/founder → high-ticket pre-qual application. **Every link UTM-tracked from video #1** so attribution exists day one. Funnel: video → first-line CTA → runconstellation.com → scan or pre-qual.

## Measurement (lead-gen weighted — not vanity)
Judge on **qualified applications + lead quality**, not views/subs. Primary: (1) tracked CTA CTR per video; (2) scan/application submissions attributed to specific videos; (3) cost-per-qualified-application by pillar and by faceless-vs-founder (to tune the 3:1 ratio + mix). Secondary (leading indicators only): first-15s hook retention (target ~65% to the 3-min mark); decision-maker subscribers as sales signals (one exec sub > 10k random viewers). **Entity-graph value:** track Constellation's *own* AI-visibility over time (brand mentions/citations across Google AI, ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Amazon) — the library is itself third-party authority content that should compound our citation footprint (eat our own cooking). **Expectation-set:** organic lead-gen compounds over ~3–6 months / 30–50 videos; the 8-week slate seeds the cluster, it isn't the harvest.
