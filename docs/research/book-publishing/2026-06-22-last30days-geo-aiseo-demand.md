# last30days — GEO / AI-SEO demand (2026-06-22)

## Verdict for our series: DEMAND-RICH, SUPPLY-SATURATED → confirms "GEO is book #2, not #1"
The topic has white-hot demand but the **book shelf is already crowded** and the "it's just SEO"
skeptic frame is loud. This validates the earlier strategic call: lead with **AI Chief of Staff
(white space)**; enter GEO **second**, only with a named framework + narrow buyer + year stamp.

## Demand signals (strong)
- **Practitioners hunting for the "how"** — the freshest Reddit thread this cycle is literally [r/digital_marketing: "What has helped you with improving visibility on ChatGPT & Google AI overview the most?"](https://www.reddit.com/r/digital_marketing/comments/1uciaoj/what_has_helped_you_with_improving_visibility_on/). People ask *how*, not *whether*.
- **Panic framing from businesses** — [HubSpot IG reel](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DZso1zcvHB9/) (8.4k views): "AI search doesn't work like Google. No second page... One answer, one brand, done. If you're not in it, you don't exist."
- **Consultants already selling the outcome** — [@denohawari](https://x.com/denohawari/status/2063156240570556772) (324 likes, 267 replies): "We just ranked a B2C SaaS brand inside ChatGPT... biggest SEO opportunity since Google."
- **Demand explicitly rising** — [@pareshmayani](https://x.com/pareshmayani/status/2068572881026773002): "SEO is [not] dead... it has rather increased the demand for working on AI SEO or AEO or GEO."
- **Money behind it** — Adobe acquired Semrush ~$1.9B framed around GEO (Nov 2025); ChatGPT 900M weekly users; Gartner projects 25% search-volume decline.

## Counter-signals (positioning risks)
- **"GEO is just SEO"** — [@semrush](https://x.com/semrush/status/2066843481553658222) + Google's 2026 guidance: AEO/GEO are *extensions* of SEO, "not separate disciplines." A book branded as a shiny-new-thing walks into this objection.
- **Book shelf already crowded** — Amazon already carries multiple GEO/AI-SEO titles, including one from an established authority: **Ross Hudgens (Siege Media), "GEO: The Definitive Guide to AI SEO"**, plus Emanuel Rose, Weiwei Hu, John Vincent Kriney, Kunal Guha, "GEO 2026" (Alex Reed). **No white space — differentiation is mandatory.**

## Funnel-vs-revenue read
The demand is for **results** (rank in ChatGPT), and the active sellers are **consultants/agencies** (the @denohawari pattern) — i.e., the value capture in this niche is **services/backend**, not book royalties. Reinforces the **funnel/authority** weighting for a GEO title specifically.
