# Jaca Sugar — Brand Spec (Test Client)

**Company**: Jaca Sugar | **Website**: jacasugar.com

## Products
- **Jaca rare sugar pouches** (16oz and 24oz) — pure allulose, core product
- **Jaca Jolt** — energy shot, caffeine & vitamins, sweetened with Jaca allulose

## Visual Identity
- **Font**: Roboto Slab (Bold=headlines, Light=body)
- **Background rule**: NO dark backgrounds EVER. Light or vibrant only.
- **Colors**:
  - Jaca Green #CCDB2A (primary)
  - Leaf Green #95C93D (primary)
  - Teal #00AF9A (accent)
  - Purple #3F2B56 (accent)
  - Red #FF4438 (accent)
  - Black #000000 (text)

## Logo
- Wordmark "JACA" with leaf/seed motif + tagline "100% Allulose Rare Sugar"
- Color variants: Green, Purple, Red, White
- WARNING: Current files have BLACK backgrounds — need transparent extraction
- Always on TRANSPARENT background
- Size: 2-2.5x larger than first instinct, readable at mobile
- Placement varies per draft

## AI Generation Rules
- **Pouch**: DO NOT feed into AI (garbles text). Generate scene only, composite real photo.
- **Jolt bottle**: OK to feed as input (simple bold graphics render correctly)
- **People**: NEVER feed into AI (distorts faces). Composite real UGC photos.
- **Text**: NEVER rely on AI-rendered text — always use Remotion

## Negative Constraints (append to EVERY prompt)
"No text overlays, no typography, no logos, no watermarks, no distorted text on packaging, no extra fingers, no deformed objects, no over-smoothed textures, no heavy HDR glow, no cartoon elements"
Food scenes add: "No unrealistic food colors, no floating objects, no impossible reflections, no duplicate items, no melted/distorted packaging"

## Language Rules
- NEVER call traditional sugar "real sugar" — Jaca IS real sugar
- Use ONLY: "traditional sugar," "conventional sugar," "the sugar we grew up with," "old school sugar"
- Approved: "Cooks, bakes, and tastes like traditional sugar"

## Product Attributes (2-3 per ad)
- **Rational**: 0 Net Carbs, 90% Fewer Calories, Non-GMO, Diabetic & Keto Friendly, Clean Ingredients
- **Emotional**: Sugar Without the Guilt, Clean Sweetness You Can Trust, Sweetness You Deserve

## 5-Element Prompt Framework
1. Subject — specific details
2. Composition — shot framing AND aspect ratio
3. Action — what's happening
4. Location — environment with light
5. Style — aesthetic + camera/lens

## Scene Templates
- Kitchen Counter (Pouch, TYPE B Lifestyle) — farmhouse, marble, golden hour, cookies
- Coffee Bar (Pouch, TYPE A Product Hero) — walnut counter, steam, morning light
- Gym/Energy (Jolt, TYPE G Bold Graphic) — bench, warehouse light, rim light
- Baking Scene (Pouch, TYPE F UGC) — overhead flat lay, flour, iPhone 15 Pro feel

## 7 Layout Types
- A: Product Hero (product 60%+, text minimal)
- B: Lifestyle Scene (product 25-35%, caption bar)
- C: Text-Wall/Testimonial (product 15-20%, text 60%+)
- D: Split-Frame (50/50, nothing crosses dividing line)
- E: Infographic/Education (product 20-30%, text 50%+)
- F: UGC-Style (product 25-35%, captions)
- G: Bold Graphic (product 40%+, text 40%+)
